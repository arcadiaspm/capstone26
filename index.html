SPM 495 — Research Seminar

The Capstone Experience

The senior capstone is the culmination of a student's academic journey in Sport Management. Each student combines coursework, internship experience, and professional development into an original research project demonstrating critical thinking and sport industry expertise.

Under faculty mentorship, students identify a research question, design a methodology, collect and analyze data, then present findings that contribute to the broader body of sport management scholarship.
